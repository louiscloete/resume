Objective Statement

Welcome to my personal space! I’m Louis Cloete, a multimedia enthusiast and a proud alumnus of the University of Pretoria, where I pursued a Bachelor of Information Science (Multimedia). My last ten years have been an incredible journey through the dynamic world of higher education, where I’ve cultivated a deep-rooted passion for multimedia production and livestreaming.

My professional path has been more than just a career; it’s a vivid tapestry of professional milestones and personal passions. I find great joy in blending technology with education to create enriching learning experiences. Orchestrating livestreams and managing video projects, especially in educational settings, has been immensely fulfilling for me.

Beyond my professional life, I am fluent in Afrikaans and English and have a diverse array of interests that keep me engaged and inspired. Fitness and health are central to my life, and I relish exploring mountain biking trails. I’m an avid fan of Formula 1, drawn to the sport’s strategic depth and competitive dynamism. Music plays a significant role in my life too, with Ludovico Einaudi’s compositions providing a stimulating soundtrack. His ability to encapsulate the essence of four seasons in a single song, especially tracks like ‘Experience’ and ‘Nuvole Bianche’ resonates deeply with me.

Community involvement is important to me, and I spend time assisting local churches with their livestreaming setups. This not only allows me to give back to society but also to connect with my roots and values.

On a more personal note, my family is the cornerstone of my life. Their support and love are the bedrock upon which I build my dreams and ambitions. They inspire me to be better every day, both in my professional endeavors and in my personal pursuits.

As I look forward to new opportunities and challenges, particularly in the innovative educational landscape of the Netherlands, I am excited about the prospect of contributing my skills and experiences. I believe in the power of collaboration and growth, and I am enthusiastic about the chance to be a part of a team that shares these values.

Please feel free to explore my resume for a more detailed look at my professional journey. I am open to discussions about how my background, skills and passions might align with and enhance the vision of your institution or project.
